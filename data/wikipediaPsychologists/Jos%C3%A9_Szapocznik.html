José Szapocznik

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

José Szapocznik, Ph.D., (born in Havana, Cuba), is a clinical psychologist of Polish-Cuban ancestry and one of the nation's leading family therapists specializing in Hispanic families. He is the developer of One-Person Family Therapy and Brief Structural Therapy. He is a leading scholar on Hispanic psychology.[1]

He is director of the Center for Family Studies at the University of Miami,[2] where he has been inducted into its Iron Arrow Honor Society. He has authored numerous scientific publications and has received numerous awards for his work on behalf of Hispanic families.
